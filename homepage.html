Major
Spurious Kapileswar Stone plaque Inscription
  • Ashoka's
  • 1st century BCE
  • Copper-Plate Charters
  • Western Ghat, near Pune
  • Kannada
Introduction
The record is on three copper plates secured with a ring and elephant seal. It is in Kannada characters of the 9th century. The language of the main portion is Sanskrit and the details of the grant are in Kannada. The record gives the genealogy of the Gangas upto Avinīta. The details about each king namely, Kȯnguṇi-mahādhirāja, Mādhava-mahādhirāja, Harivarma-mahādhirāja, Vishṇugōpamahādhirāja, Mādhava-Mahādhirāja and Avinīta agree with most of the copper plate records of the Gȧngas hitherto discovered.
